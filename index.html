Who are We?

Singapore functional programmers is a small group of programmers who share a passion in functional programming. Most of us are based in Singapore and we hold events on a regular basis.

Our meet-ups are held once every 2 months, it does have a slant towards Haskell as Haskellers happen to dominate in numbers. When we meet, we talk about functional programming, functional programming and functional programming! You may be updated about our meet-up details through joining our mailing list. Come join the fun!
